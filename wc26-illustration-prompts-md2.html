WC2026 · Illustration Prompts
Matchday 2 · All 24 Games

24 matches · June 18–23, 2026 · Woodblock / linocut print style

Consistent style across all 24 illustrations — DIFFERENT from Matchday 1

· Background: deep forest green (#0B1E0D) — matches the app background exactly

· Style: woodblock print / linocut — bold carved strokes, rough-hewn texture, visible wood grain and gouge marks, heavy black outlines, areas of dense ink coverage

· Colors: 2–3 earthy spot colors per illustration, drawn from each team's national palette (burnt sienna, ochre, raw umber, deep indigo, etc.)

· Composition: two national symbols confronting each other, one per country, each commanding their half

· Format: horizontal, 3:2 ratio (e.g. 1500×1000px), no text

· Mood: raw and powerful — like a vintage Japanese woodblock print or a 1960s protest print

· Key contrast vs MD1: where MD1 uses smooth halftone grain + slight ink misregistration, MD2 uses carved wood texture, gouged lines, and heavy black contour

File naming convention: save as match-{group}-{home abbr}-{away abbr}.png and drop in /quiniela/public/
Example: match-a-cze-rsa.png for Czech Republic vs South Africa (Group A Matchday 2)
