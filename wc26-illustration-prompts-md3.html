Consistent style across all 24 illustrations — same risograph aesthetic as Matchday 1

· Background: deep forest green (#0B1E0D) — identical to MD1, consistent with the app background

· Style: risograph screenprint — flat spot colors, visible halftone grain, slight ink misregistration, bold graphic energy

· Technique words to use: "flat spot colors, visible halftone grain, slight ink misregistration" — NOT woodblock, linocut, engraving, or travel poster

· Colors: 2–3 rich spot colors per illustration drawn from each team's national palette

· Subjects: real creatures and symbols with specific poses, attitudes, and decorative context — NOT flat geometric silhouettes

· Composition: two national symbols facing each other across the horizontal frame, each commanding their half

· Format: horizontal, 3:2 ratio (1500×1000px), no text

· Mood: bold, graphic, vintage tournament poster energy — same as MD1

File naming convention: save as match-{group}-{home abbr}-{away abbr}.png and drop in /quiniela/public/
