Consistent style across all 24 illustrations — warm background variant of Matchday 2

· Background: warm dark olive-green (#1C3010) — slightly warmer and lighter than MD1/MD2's cool forest green (#0B1E0D), creates a distinct "final chapter" feel while staying in the same family

· Style: woodblock print / linocut — identical technique to MD2: bold carved strokes, rough-hewn texture, visible wood grain and gouge marks, heavy black outlines, dense ink coverage

· Colors: 2–3 earthy spot colors per illustration, drawn from each team's national palette (same palette logic as MD2)

· Composition: two national symbols confronting each other, one per country, each commanding their half

· Format: horizontal, 3:2 ratio (e.g. 1500×1000px), no text

· Mood: raw and dramatic — same vintage Japanese woodblock / 1960s protest print energy as MD2

· Key contrast vs MD2: only the background shifts from cool forest green to warm olive-green — everything else is identical, making the three matchdays feel like a coherent series

File naming convention: save as match-{group}-{home abbr}-{away abbr}.png and drop in /quiniela/public/
Example: match-a-cze-mex.png for Czech Republic vs Mexico (Group A Matchday 3)
