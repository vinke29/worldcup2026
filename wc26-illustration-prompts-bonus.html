Bonus Questions · 7 Awards

7 bonus question cards · Same aesthetic as match illustrations · Single-subject compositions

Consistent style — matches the game card illustrations exactly

· Background: deep forest green (#0B1E0D) — matches the app background exactly

· Style: risograph / silk-screen print — flat spot colors, visible halftone grain, slight ink misregistration gives a handmade feel

· Colors: 2–3 spot colors max per illustration, chosen to match each award's mood and color symbolism

· Composition: single dominant subject, centered or slightly off-center, commanding the full frame — no match duality here

· Format: horizontal, 3:2 ratio — generate at exactly 1500×1000px

· Mood: bold, graphic, collector's-item energy — like a vintage poster for a specific award or moment

· No text, no numbers, no letters, no logos of real brands

· The subject should feel iconic and timeless — not tied to any specific player or team

Key difference from match cards: Match cards have two animals facing off (one per team). Bonus cards have a single iconic subject filling the frame — a trophy, a creature, a dramatic moment. Think of them as award posters, not match posters. The subject should be immediately recognizable as representing the award even without text.
File naming: save as bonus-{key}.png and drop in /public/
